About Me

Hi, I'm Charlie. I'm from Springfield, Missouri. I am a senior at Mizzou. I am in the Environmental Science program, on track to graduate in 2024. I love the environment and doing whatever I can to protect the natural world. My volunteer time at the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) has taught me we still have much to learn and teach about climate change.
In the future, I hope to shape environmentalist policy that will appeal to both consumer and business. For fun, I enjoy reading and gaming. I also love traveling and hiking. Seeing new parts of the world and taking in their natural beauty motivates me to preserve the planet, through both my own action and helping others through theirs.
